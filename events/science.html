About Science Exhibition

Join us for an inspiring Science Exhibition where curiosity sparks innovation! Students will present creative experiments, working models, and scientific ideas that address real-world challenges. Whether you're a budding scientist or simply passionate about discovery, this exhibition is the perfect platform to explore, learn, and showcase the wonders of science.

A panel of esteemed judges from academic and scientific institutions will evaluate projects based on creativity, scientific accuracy, and real-world relevance. Outstanding exhibits will be awarded exciting prizes, along with opportunities for recognition and mentorship from industry experts.
