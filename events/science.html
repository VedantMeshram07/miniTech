Hackathon 2025

A 24-hour coding marathon that challenges teams to build innovative tech solutions to real-world problems.

Prize Pool ₹30,000
Date 14th–15th October 2025
Participation Team Event
Team Size 2–4 members

Rules & Guidelines

  • Each team must consist of 2 to 4 members.
  • Projects must be completed within the given 24-hour period.
  • Use of AI and open-source tools is encouraged.
  • Judging will be based on innovation, impact, and technical quality.
  • All team members must be present during the event.
  • Final presentation is mandatory for all participating teams.

About the Event

Hackathon 2025 is the ultimate coding challenge that brings together the brightest minds in technology. Participants will brainstorm, prototype, and present their projects to a panel of judges from leading tech companies. This intensive 24-hour event is designed to push your creativity and technical skills to the limit.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or a passionate beginner, this hackathon offers an incredible opportunity to learn, network, and showcase your talents. Exciting prizes, internship opportunities, and industry recognition await the winners. Join us for an unforgettable journey of innovation and collaboration!
