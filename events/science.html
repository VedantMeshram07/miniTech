Hackathon 2025

Hackathon 2025 is a 24-hour coding marathon that challenges teams to build innovative tech solutions to real-world problems. Participants will brainstorm, prototype, and present their projects to a panel of judges from the tech industry. Exciting prizes and internship opportunities await the winners!

Prize Pool ₹30,000
Date 14th–15th October 2025
Participation Team Event
Team Size 2–4 members

Rules & Guidelines

  • Each team must consist of 2 to 4 members.
  • Projects must be completed within the given 24-hour period.
  • Use of AI and open-source tools is encouraged.
  • Judging will be based on innovation, impact, and technical quality.

About Hackathon 2025

Join us for an exhilarating 24-hour coding marathon where innovation meets implementation. Teams will work together to solve real-world problems using cutting-edge technology. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your coding journey, this hackathon offers the perfect platform to showcase your skills, learn from peers, and potentially launch the next big idea.

Our expert panel of judges from leading tech companies will evaluate projects based on innovation, technical excellence, and real-world impact. Winners will receive substantial cash prizes and may be offered internship opportunities at prestigious organizations.
